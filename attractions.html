Local Attractions

Never get bored when you stay at the Royal Suites. Local attactions will keep you busy and entertained during your entire stay at our hotel.

North Point Mall

North Point is a favorite shopping destination in Atlanta’s top suburb, Alpharetta. The mall features a desirable shopping and dining collection, including brands and concepts such as Aerie, Sephora, Macy’s, Cheesecake Factory, Victoria’s Secret, California Pizza Kitchen, Lego, Build-A-Bear, H&M, and more!

Alpharetta Downtown

Downtown Alpharetta is where we gather for a meal, shop at unique boutiques and specialty stores, enjoy live music or relax with a cup of coffee and our furry friend. We invite you to discover the people and places that make the District so special!

Avalon

Avalon is a new experience in the timeless art of living well. In the heart of Alpharetta, this sustainably designed, 86-acre community features more than 500,000 square feet of retail, a 12-screen all premium theater, conference center and full-service hotel, Class A office, single-family residences and luxury rental homes. Avalon infuses resort-level hospitality throughout a walkable, seamlessly connected community of shopping, dining, entertainment, living and working.

Ameris Bank Amphitheatre

Ameris Bank Amphitheatre in Alpharetta, Georgia is a 12,000 seat, state-of-the-art venue providing an exceptional backdrop for some of the biggest names in country, rock and top 40. Let the musical waves overtake you in this beautiful park-like setting.

Topgolf

Topgolf is a sports entertainment complex that features an inclusive, high-tech golf game that everyone can enjoy. Paired with an outstanding food and beverage menu, climate-controlled hitting bays and music, Topgolf at Alpharetta has an energetic hum that you can feel right when you walk through the door.

Wills Park

Local park in Alpharetta, Georgia. Recreation options from walking trails, ball fields & swimming to an equestrian arena & dog park. Join for fun activities for families and children, or enjoy exploring through the great outdoors.
